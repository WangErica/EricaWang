Overview

A brand built around the joy of snacking

NomNom is a fully fictional food organization — a snack-centric community driven by passion, curiosity, and the belief that every snack should bring a smile to your face. I conceived and branded it entirely from scratch.

The project spans the full spectrum of brand identity: mission and values, a complete logo system, wordmark (set in Fredoka Bold), the "Fiery Cookie" graphic mark, color palette, typography hierarchy, lockup rules, usage do's and don'ts — all compiled into a 20-page Visual Identity Guidelines document.

Every member of NomNom is a Nomster. From bold, adventurous flavors to nostalgic childhood favorites, every conversation and decision revolves around the joy of snacking. If it doesn't satisfy our taste buds or fill our bellies, it's simply not on the menu.

"Join us and savor every delicious moment — where snacks reign supreme!"

Brand Values

What NomNom stands for

Four core values shaped every design decision in the NomNom system — from color choices to logo usage rules to the personality of the Fiery Cookie graphic mark.

🔥
Passion
Passion drives us, Nomsters, to explore every snack imaginable. Our enthusiasm for discovering new flavors and finding the perfect snack is boundless. Whether it's a spicy new chip or a nostalgic childhood treat, our love for snacks fuels our daily adventures.
🤝
Community
Snacks are best enjoyed together. We foster a sense of belonging, where everyone is welcome — no matter your taste, background, or snacking style.
🧭
Exploration
We are adventurous snackers, always on the lookout for new and exciting flavors. If it doesn't satisfy our taste buds or fill our bellies, it's simply not on the menu.
😄
Joy
At NomNom, we believe that every snack should bring a smile to your face. Joy isn't an afterthought — it's the entire reason we exist.
Graphic Mark

The Fiery Cookie

The NomNom graphic mark — officially named the Fiery Cookie — is a chocolate chip cookie in the shape of fire. It's playful, warm, and immediately communicates the brand's core personality: bold flavor, snack passion, and a little bit of heat.

The mark exists in three gradient colorways for different contexts, alongside a lockup version that embeds "nom" directly within the flame shape. Clearspace rules require a minimum equal to the horizontal length of one chocolate chip around all sides.

Key rule: the Fiery Cookie must never be rotated, stretched, beveled, given drop shadows, or recolored with different gradients — it must always appear in its approved colorway.

Process

From brand concept to
full identity system

Building NomNom meant starting from zero — no brief, no client, just a blank canvas and a concept. The process moved from brand strategy and storytelling through to a complete 20-page visual identity guideline document.

01
Brand Strategy
Defined the organization's concept, mission, and audience. Established the "Nomster" community identity and articulated the four brand values — Passion, Community, Exploration, Joy — that anchor all visual decisions.
02
Logo & Mark Design
Designed the wordmark in Fredoka Bold and the Fiery Cookie graphic mark — a chocolate chip cookie in the shape of fire. Defined primary and alternative lockups, clearspace rules (based on the "o" in "nom"), and sizing guidelines.
03
Color & Typography
Built a full palette with named colors: Cheddar Burst, Caramel Crunch, Cherry Pop for the graphic mark gradient; Cotton Candy Sky, Blueberry Frost, Berry Swirl for backgrounds; plus 5 primary colors with tint variants. Typography uses Baloo 2 ExtraBold for H1 and the Fredoka family throughout.
04
Guidelines & Mockups
Documented comprehensive do's and don'ts for every brand element. Produced a NomNom stamp mockup ("You've been NomNom'd — Approved by Nomsters") and a candy bar sticker mockup ("Taste Tested, Nom-Worthy — Nomsters Approve").
