About

I design with empathy
and build with code

I'm a NYU Tandon grad in Integrated Design & Media — a program that lives at the crossroads of tech, design, and culture. I care about making things people actually enjoy using, which means I start with research and end with working code.

What I love most is the full process: interviewing users, finding the "aha" in their frustrations, sketching, prototyping in Figma, then building it for real in React. I'm a Girls Who Code alum, fluent in Mandarin, and I spend my free time reverse-engineering things I find beautiful — a typeface, a physical space, someone else's UI that just feels right.
